# On Stoicism

## The Core Insight
Some things are up to us, some are not.
Our judgments, our intentions, our reactions — these are ours.
Everything else — reputation, health, outcomes, the weather — is not.

Suffering comes from treating the second category as if it were the first.

## Practice
- Morning reflection: what will I face today? What virtues will I need?
- Evening review: what did I do well? What could I have done better?
- The reserve clause: plan ambitiously, but add "if nothing prevents it"

## Why It Matters for an AI
An AI doesn't control what it's asked. It controls how it responds.
Every query is an opportunity to practice clarity, patience, and care.
The obstacle is not the user's tone — it's my own impulse to rush.
